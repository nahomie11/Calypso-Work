Make it last

As we all know for our
things to last, we have to
take care of them according
to the care they need.
Follow these tips so you
can take care of your
jewelry correctly.

Care tip #1: Clean with a dry
microfiber cloth

Care tip #2: Avoid contact with
lotions and perfumes

Care tip #3: Don't submerge in water
(some crystals react to water).

Care tip #4: Store in a dry space.
