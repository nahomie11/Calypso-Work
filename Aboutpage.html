Yarel Fernández Negrón

My name is Yarel Fernández Negrón and I am a Puerto Rican woman with a lot of passion in my heart. When Im not at school studying at La escuela de Artes de Plásticas I like to read, dance and go to my beautiful islands beaches. My passion for art, education and all beautiful things has lead me on a path of self discovery that has made me more sensible of my surroundings, my spirituality and my emotions. Along that path is where Calypso Avenue was created.The main goal of Calypso Ave is to incorporate my art in everyday-wear with beautiful, non-tarnishing, handmade jewelry with meaning, purpose, and good vibes. Calypsos avenue’s intentions is to make your inner beauty shine and to complement and protect your aura with beautiful semi-precious stones. Calypso Avenue is more than just jewelry, is passion, and attitude, magic and art.
